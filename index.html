Frequently Asked Questions


How can I find drivers or passengers to share a ride with?

All you have to do is click on "search for a journey" and fill in the information requested. The routes will then be proposed to you.

What security measures are in place on your platform?

Our users' security is of course our top priority. We check the identity of all users with ID verification and picture recognition. We work with a cybersecurity platform to protect all the data. We also work with an operational security platform that enables us to assist users quickly in the event of problems.

Can I share a ride with friends or colleagues?

We recommend that you also do this with people you know by registering on the application. It's extra security and it could also help people get home safely.

How does the user rating system work?

Users are rated by those with whom they have already shared a journey. This makes for a safe and efficient rating system.
